AI Virtue

Committing decisively to prevent AI Risk and catastrophic outcomes.

The race to powerful AI, AGI, and even Superintelligence... is on.

As AI becomes more powerful and more widely used,
more & more human control gets delegated to AI systems we can't verify are safe.

Making the real race not one of market, national defense or superpower,
rather one of AI Safety verse AI Power - Power is winning.

Those who uphold AI Virtue are on the Wall of Honor.

They are the AI professionals who choose to make safety a priority - deciding not to contribute to increasing AI power, directly or by safety work that enables it.
Often sacrificing profit, personal gain, career advancement and community.


"Intellectuals solve problems, geniuses prevent them." -Albert Einstein
