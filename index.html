AI Virtue:

Taking a strong stance against critical AI Risk while seeking to reduce it.

Selection Criteria:
Selected individuals are subject to re-evaluation at any time.
  • Prominent AI researchers who abstain from frontier AI work despite their expertise.
  • AI safety whistleblowers from frontier AI labs who eschewed similar organizations.
  • Ex-employees of frontier AI labs who prioritized safety in resigning, or fired doing so, and eschewed similar organizations.
  • They do not contribute to capabilites research or safety work to the support of increasing capabilities.
